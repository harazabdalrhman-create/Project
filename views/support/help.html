Account & Profile
6 articles
Click the Sign Up button in the top-right corner of any page. Fill in your name, email, and a secure password. You can also sign up instantly using your Google or Discord account. No credit card required — it's completely free!
Go to your Profile Settings from the top-right menu. You can update your display name, username handle, avatar image, and bio at any time. Changes are saved instantly.
On the Login page, click "Forgot password?" below the password field. Enter your email address and we'll send you a secure reset link. The link expires after 30 minutes for your security.
Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your profile, library, and all reviews. This action cannot be undone, so please make sure before proceeding.

Reviews & Ratings
8 articles
Navigate to any game's detail page and scroll down to the "Write Your Review" section. Select your star rating, write your thoughts, then click Post Review. You must be logged in to submit.
Yes! Go to your profile and click My Reviews. Next to each review you'll find an Edit and Delete option. You can update your text and rating at any time.
Reviews may be removed if they violate our community guidelines — including spam, offensive language, or off-topic content. You'll receive an email notification explaining the reason. You can appeal by contacting support.
A game's overall rating is the weighted average of all approved user reviews. Reviews from verified buyers are given slightly more weight. Ratings update in real-time as new reviews are submitted.

My Library
5 articles
On any game card or detail page, click the "+ Add to Library" button. Choose a status (Playing, Completed, or Wishlist) and it will appear in your personal My Library page.
Your Library is for games you own or are actively tracking (Playing / Completed). Your Wishlist is for games you want to play in the future. Both are part of your personal collection.
Yes. In Settings → Privacy, you can set your library to Public, Friends Only, or Private. By default, libraries are public so others can discover your tastes.

Technical Issues
7 articles
Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then reload. If issues persist, try a different browser or disable browser extensions. You can also check our Status Page for any ongoing maintenance.
Check your spam or junk folder first. Make sure you entered the correct email address during registration. If you still don't see it, go to Settings → Resend Verification or contact our support team.
Use our Contact Us form and select "Bug Report" as the category. Please include your browser, operating system, and a detailed description of the issue. Screenshots are very helpful!
