Your privacy matters. Gaming Hub is committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we collect and use it. This policy explains everything in plain language.

Section 01
Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account, write reviews, or interact with the platform, as well as data generated automatically when you use our services.

Information you provide:

  • Account details — name, username, email address, and password (stored encrypted)
  • Profile information — avatar, bio, gaming preferences, and favourite genres
  • User-generated content — reviews, ratings, comments, and game library entries
  • Communications — messages you send to our support team

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, search queries, and session duration
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and IP address
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 3)
Section 02
How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to operate and improve Gaming Hub, personalise your experience, and keep you informed about relevant updates.

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our platform and features
  • To personalise game recommendations based on your ratings and library
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
  • To send optional marketing emails if you opt in (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data to serve you targeted advertising from external ad networks.

Section 03
Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use Gaming Hub.

  • Essential cookies — Required for the site to function (login sessions, security tokens)
  • Preference cookies — Remember your settings (theme, language, display preferences)
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand traffic and improve the platform (anonymised data)

You can manage or disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect some features.

Section 04
Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — Trusted third parties who help us operate (hosting, email delivery, analytics). They are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements — When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business transfers — If Gaming Hub merges or is acquired, your data may transfer. We'll notify you beforehand.
  • Your consent — Any other sharing requires your explicit permission
Section 05
Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain-text passwords
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Two-factor authentication available for all accounts

While we take strong precautions, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and enable 2FA on your account.

Section 06
Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Ask us to fix any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to certain types of processing (e.g., marketing)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@gaminghub.com or use our Contact Us form. We'll respond within 30 days.

Section 07
Children's Privacy

Gaming Hub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us personal data, please contact us immediately at privacy@gaminghub.com and we will delete the information promptly.

Section 08
Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Reviews and public content may be retained in anonymised form after deletion
  • Log and analytics data is retained for a maximum of 24 months
  • Backups are permanently deleted within 90 days of account deletion
Section 09
Contact & Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by email and by posting a notice on our platform. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact our Data Privacy team:
